What We Offer

Complete Pest Solutions

Every treatment uses government-approved, eco-friendly products applied by trained technicians. All services include professional support and service warranty.

🐜 Termite Control Most Popular

Advanced termite treatment for homes, offices and commercial buildings. Our pre and post-construction solutions create a protective barrier that prevents termite attacks on furniture, doors, windows and wooden structures.

🪳 Cockroach Control

We use gel bait treatment and residual spray techniques to eliminate cockroaches from kitchens, bathrooms and hidden areas. Safe, odourless and highly effective for residential and commercial properties.

🛏️ Bed Bug Control Guaranteed

Complete bed bug eradication using specialized treatment methods for mattresses, beds, sofas and furniture. Follow-up inspections ensure maximum effectiveness and long-term protection.

🐭 Rodent (Rat) Control

Effective rat and mouse control using bait stations, trapping systems and entry-point sealing techniques. Designed to eliminate infestations while maintaining safety for families and pets.

🏠 Anti-Termite Pipeline System

Specialized anti-termite piping systems installed in buildings to provide long-term termite prevention. Allows periodic treatment without damaging floors or structures.

🐜 Ant Control

Targeted baiting and barrier treatments designed to destroy ant colonies and prevent future infestations. Effective against black ants, red ants and other common species.

🐝 Honey Bee & Insect Control

Safe removal of bee hives and treatment for flying and crawling insects. Our technicians ensure minimal disturbance while protecting residents and surrounding property.

🏗️ Pre-Construction Termite Treatment

Soil treatment before construction creates a strong termite-resistant barrier beneath the building foundation, protecting the property for years to come.

🦎 Lizard & Spider Control

Professional treatment to reduce lizards, spiders and their food sources. Ideal for homes, offices, warehouses and commercial establishments where crawling pests are a concern.

🛡️ General Pest Control

Comprehensive pest management service covering cockroaches, ants, spiders, silverfish, mosquitoes and other common household pests. Recommended for routine protection and maintenance.
